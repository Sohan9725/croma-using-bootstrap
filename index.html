Croma is India's first large format specialist electronics retail store. Founded in 2006, Croma brings under one roof, multi-brand digital gadgets and home electronics products.


From large appliances such as Air conditioner,Refrigeator , and Televisions to handy devices such as Laptops, Mobile phones, and Cameras, Croma stocks over 22,000 products from over 500 brands in more than 248 stores across 80+ major cities.


Croma brings alive the promise of a ‘Brighter Every Day’ with a world-class ambience to shop both in-store and online on croma.com while also creating a seamless omnichannel shopping experience that lets you enjoy the best of both, online and offline worlds.


You’ll always be spoilt for choice at Croma but will never be left confused. Experts at our stores will help you find the right appliance or gadget for your needs. Simply #AskCromaExperts or find the right electronics and appliances by yourself online with these Croma buying guides, for everything from gaming consoles, microwaves, desktops, air coolers, smart home devices, and more.


Whether you’re setting up a new home or looking to upgrade, our range of appliances from Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Godrej, and IFB among others will ensure you go home with the best appliance from the best brands.


Croma’s own-label products – ACs, Refrigerators, Televisions, Washing Machines, and other kitchen appliances – aren’t just among the best in their respective categories, they’re also competitively priced.



Be productive at work with our range of Windows and Chromebook laptops from HP, Dell, and Lenovo among others, as well as MacBook machines from Apple. Or carry your entertainment in your bag with iPad and Surface or Android tablets from Lenovo, Samsung, and others.
